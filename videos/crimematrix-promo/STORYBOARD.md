## Video direction

- **palette system:** White / cool-gray canvas (`bg` `#ffffff`), deep navy ink (`text` `#0f172a`), single badge-gold accent (`primary` `#f59e0b`). Soft gold-tinted cards (`card-bg` / `border`). Muted slate for secondary (`text-muted` `#475569`). Emerald / red only for risk-positive / risk-negative chips. No purple glow, no neon cyberpunk.
- **type:** Plus Jakarta Sans for display + body (Space Grotesk chrome roles remapped to Plus Jakarta Sans in practice). JetBrains Mono for FIR IDs, confidence %, data labels.
- **motion defaults + shot model:** Every frame is a directed shot: entrance → development → settle. Default ease `power2.out` / `power3.out`. Idle budget: one ambient sine breath OR one slow push — never both competing. Caption keep-out: bottom ~17% clear.
- **negative list:** No purple-on-white AI cliché; no enter-then-freeze slideshow; no screensaver (everything floating independently); no hard drop-shadow stacks; no fake browser chrome with traffic lights.
- **stillness allocation:** Frame 3 (brand lockup hold) and Frame 12 (tagline CTA hold) may settle still. Every other frame develops mid-shot.
